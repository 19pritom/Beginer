Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

About the Department

The Department of Computer Application established in the year of 2012, under the School of Technology, NEHU, funded by MHRD, Govt. of India, with a prime focus to impart quality, career/research-oriented course in computer application to produce best possible manpower in the field.

Master of Computer Applications is a 2-year (4-semester) Master's Degree in applied computer science. The core part of the curriculum embodies theoretical and practical knowledge basic to the industry/academic/research. In addition to the core courses the programme also incorporates current and emerging technologies relevant to the field of computer application. To tackle theoretical and real life problems, the syllabus/ coursework has been designed by balancing various core, specialized and elective courses. The syllabus also includes several practical papers and challenging projects etc. The M.C.A. curriculum is also enriched with management papers to give an exposure to student in the field of corporate management.
